From a Local Store to a Global Electronics Network


What started as a single electronics store in Helwan has grown into a globally connected platform serving customers across borders. At EG ZONE MART, we’ve built our journey on trust, quality, and a passion for technology — expanding our reach while staying true to our roots. We continue to evolve, bringing the latest electronics and smart solutions to people everywhere.

1997:

EG ZONE MART began as a small electronics shop in Helwan, Cairo, specializing in essential devices and repair services for the local community.

1998:

With growing trust, the store expanded to central Cairo and served additional areas in southern Cairo.

2001:

Opened a new branch in Downtown Cairo, offering modern gadgets and accessories to a wider customer base.

2002:

Expanded services to major Egyptian cities, including Alexandria, Mansoura, and Assiut, becoming a nationwide name in consumer electronics.

2005:

Formed strategic partnerships with top tech brands and launched a regional delivery network covering all Egyptian governorates.

2005:

Entered the Arab market, starting with GCC countries and North Africa, expanding operations and logistics to support cross-border orders.

2009:

Launched the first version of EG ZONE MART's online store, offering nationwide e-commerce and secure payment options.

2010:

Transformed into a full-fledged global e-commerce platform with international shipping, multi-language support, and a growing global customer base.

2015:

Began expanding into Europe and the Americas, increasing demand for high-quality electronics.

2020:

Started accepting digital currencies, enhancing global accessibility and reaching a broader audience.

2025:

Now serving over a billion users annually from all races and regions, from Russia in the West to the United States in the East, leading the global e-commerce industry.
